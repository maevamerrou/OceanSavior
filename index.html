Instructions

In order to clean the ocean from the trash human produce, our character, the diver Melissa, has to catch as many trash elements as possible in her net. Help her to succeed. Naturally, you must avoid to catch fishes and other animals as the sea is their enviroment. On the bottom there is a barrier reef composed of corals, but occasionnaly you will find a tire, if you manage to catch it you will gain bonus points, but if you fail and touch the corals or the sand, you lose! Use the click of your mouse to keep Melissa up.
